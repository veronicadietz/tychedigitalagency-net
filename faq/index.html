Understanding the Three Offers

What is the difference between the Free Strategy Call and the Direction Session?

These are two completely different things and it is important to choose the right one.

The Free Strategy Call is a discovery and fit conversation for businesses that are interested in done-for-you implementation. It is free. We talk through what you need, whether Tyche is the right fit, and what working together would look like. It is not a diagnostic and it does not produce a map of next moves.

The Direction Session is a paid, 60-minute diagnostic session. It costs $500. We go deep on your business, locate the structural issue generating most of your downstream problems, and produce a written summary with prioritized next moves. You can use that summary to implement yourself, hire Tyche, or hire someone else. There is no obligation to continue.

If you are not sure which you need: if you want clarity on what is wrong, book a Direction Session. If you already know you want done-for-you support and want to discuss fit, book the Free Strategy Call.

Is the Free Strategy Call actually free?

Yes. The Free Strategy Call is completely free. There is no credit card required to book, no fee, and no obligation after the call. It is a conversation about whether Tyche is the right fit for your done-for-you implementation needs.

What is Check Your Alignment?

Check Your Alignment is a free self-diagnostic. You answer a short series of questions about your business and receive a PDF by email that identifies where to focus right now. It is designed to help you narrow your efforts to the few actions that actually move revenue, so you stop spreading yourself too thin.

It takes about five minutes and requires no call, no credit card, and no follow-up from Tyche unless you want it. It feeds into an email sequence that offers the Direction Session and Free Strategy Call as next steps based on what your results indicate.

Which should I start with?

Use this as a guide:

Start with Check Your Alignment if you are not sure what is wrong or where to focus. It is free and takes five minutes.

Direction Session

What happens in a Direction Session?

After booking you receive a short intake form. We review it before the session so no paid time is spent on background. During the 60-minute session we identify the primary structural issue generating most of your downstream problems, clarify what is actually causing the drag, and map next moves in priority order. Within 24 hours of the session you receive a written summary of everything we covered.

Do I need to buy anything else after a Direction Session?

No. The Direction Session is complete on its own. Some clients leave and implement everything themselves. Others decide they want Tyche to handle the implementation. That decision is entirely yours and there is no pitch or pressure at the end of the session.

How is a Direction Session different from coaching?

Coaching typically focuses on mindset, accountability, and ongoing support. A Direction Session is a diagnostic. We are looking for what is structurally wrong, not coaching you through how you feel about it. The output is a specific identification of the load-bearing issue and a prioritized set of next actions, not a program or ongoing relationship.

Should I do a Direction Session before done-for-you implementation?

Not always, but often yes. If you already have clear clarity on what needs to be built, a Direction Session may not be necessary before starting implementation. Book the Free Strategy Call and we will tell you honestly whether we think you need one first.

If you are not yet clear on what the problem is or what needs to be built, a Direction Session should come first. Building the wrong thing is more expensive than taking the time to find out what is actually needed.

Free Strategy Call

What is the Free Strategy Call for?

The Free Strategy Call is specifically for businesses that are considering done-for-you implementation work with Tyche. It is a discovery conversation to discuss what you need, assess fit, and talk through what working together would look like.

It is not a diagnostic session. If you want a proper diagnostic, book a Direction Session instead.

What happens after the Free Strategy Call?

If it is a clear fit, we outline a scope and next steps. If it is not a fit, we tell you honestly and may point you toward a Direction Session or another resource. There is no obligation to move forward after the call.

Check Your Alignment

What do I receive after completing Check Your Alignment?

You receive a PDF by email that identifies the primary area of your business creating the most drag and gives you a prioritized starting point. You also receive a short email sequence that shares resources and offers the Direction Session and Free Strategy Call as logical next steps based on what your results indicate.

Is there a sales call after I submit the form?

No. Check Your Alignment is self-service. You complete it, you receive your PDF, and you decide what to do next. Tyche will not call you. You may receive a follow-up email sequence with resources and offers, but there is no pressure and you can opt out at any time.
