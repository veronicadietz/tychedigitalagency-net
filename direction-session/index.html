What This Session Is

A diagnostic, not
a strategy session.

Most strategy sessions end with a list of things to do. A Direction Session ends with a specific identification of what is structurally wrong and why, so you know exactly which thing to fix first and what to stop doing entirely.

You leave knowing what is wrong, what to do about it, and in what order. That is the product.

  • Identification of the primary load-bearing issue in your business

  • No follow-up retainer required or implied
